Our Collections are made up of colorful artisan produced home decor, jewelry and wardrobe accents. They are inspired by the beauty of Red Rock sunsets and the fresh air, sunshine and cool evenings of high desert living. They represent the copper mining that put Jerome on the map as well as the emerging wine industry in the Verde Valley.
