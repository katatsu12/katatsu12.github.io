Just Us Girls is a lifestyle boutique that carries functional, comfortable and unique women's clothing and accessories. Many of our brands are exclusive to Just Us Girls and are made of eco friendly fabrics such as silk, bamboo and organic cotton. We specialize in dresses and skirts for all your travel, event and every day needs! We also sell Lovelocks to commemorate your Sedona memories by engraving a lock to hang on the Lovelock gate outside our boutique or take it home as a souvenir of Sedona.
